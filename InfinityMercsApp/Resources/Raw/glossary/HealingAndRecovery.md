If a WIP roll for healing/repairing a troop is failed, <u>no further W/STR are lost in the process</u>. This applies to all relevant rolls (Doctor, Engineer, Medikit, Regeneration). 

Furthermore, **at the start of each round after the Order Count phase**, the acting player makes a **PH-6** roll for each of his/her unconscious units on the field. If successful, the unit is healed by 1 W/STR, and can act in the same turn (though the related order is not generated).
