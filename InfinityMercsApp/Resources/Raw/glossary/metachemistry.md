# Metachemistry - Recruit Skill

[Obligatory](https://infinitythewiki.com/Labels#Obligatory)

## Effects

* When a player first purchases a unit with Metachemistry they must make a public roll on the metachemistry table. Whatever result they get, they gain **permanently**. 
* Metachemistry (Reroll) allows for a reroll as well.
* Bonuses from Metachemistry are always applied AFTER all other stats such as from armor or transmutation states. 
    * Example: Rolling +1 ARM and Immunity (ARM) on a Kiel-Saan who had bought a light armor (and therefore has armor capped at 2) would allow that Kiel-Saan to have 3 ARM in the starting state, and 1 ARM in the 2nd state as well.
