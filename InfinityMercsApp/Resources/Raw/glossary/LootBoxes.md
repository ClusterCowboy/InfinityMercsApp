# GAINING LOOT BOXES
Organizers can introduce loot boxes in a variety of ways. They can be season prizes, offered for doing special tasks, or may appear in shops, events, or other game systems as the rules are updated.

# ROLLING A LOOT BOX

When rolling a lootbox, first roll the weapon type. To do so select a number of dice equal to or less than the number listed for the lootbox.  A player may select up to 4 dice and subtract that number from the loot boxes remaining dice. Only the last dice is rolled and the others are treated as 6s and the resulting roll is found in the Weapons tab of the Loot sheet. 

Then, the player may take any remaining dice and use them for upgrades. They follow the same process selecting up to a maximum of 4 each time and then rolling for an upgrade. This process can be repeated so long as the loot box has not exhausted all of its dice.

If any illegal upgrade (or a duplicate upgrade) is ever rolled due to restrictions or some other rule, such as rolling Continuous after rolling a Non-Lethal buff, the dice are rerolled until a legal buff is earned. 

Players are encouraged to think about the combinations of buffs gained to ensure they create legal combinations as many illegal ones will naturally arise.
