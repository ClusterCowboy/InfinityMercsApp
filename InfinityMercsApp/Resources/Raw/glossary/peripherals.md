The Peripheral skill is reworked with the exception of Peripheral Servants and Ancillary. Peripheral Servants/Ancillary follow all normal rules for Peripherals while all other Peripherals and their controllers gain the following benefits. For clarification, “own peripheral” refers to the peripheral the controller started with or gained otherwise and only that specific peripheral.

## Controllers

* Gain the FT Master (own peripheral) skill
* May form and join a Fireteam with their own peripheral
* Counts as their own peripheral for the purposes of fireteam bonuses
* Does not need to spend an order or command token to reform a fireteam with their own peripheral

## Peripherals
* May gain XP, level ups, equipment and otherwise counts as and behaves as a normal trooper on the roster.
* Gains the Irregular classification, providing an irregular order to the pool instead of a regular one.
* Counts as their own controller for the purposes of fireteam bonuses
