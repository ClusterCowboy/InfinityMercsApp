# Strategos L1 - Automatic Skill

[Optional](https://infinitythewiki.com/Labels#Optional)

## Requirements

* In order to benefit from this Level of Strategos, the user must be the army's Lieutenant.

## Effects

* During the Deployment Phase, you may set aside two Troopers and their Peripherals to deploy after your opponent instead of the usual one.


# Strategos L2 - Automatic Skill

[Optional](https://infinitythewiki.com/Labels#Optional)

## Requirements

* In order to benefit from this Level of Strategos, the user must be the army's Lieutenant.

## Effects

* During the Deployment Phase, you may set aside two Troopers and their Peripherals to deploy after your opponent instead of the usual one.
* At the beginning of your Active Turn, during your Order Count, replace any Special Lieutenant Orders with Regular Orders, adding them to the Order Pool of the Lieutenant's Combat Group.
