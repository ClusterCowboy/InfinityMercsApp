A Wound Skill refers to the total punishment a unit can take. This incorporates both the Wounds attribute of a unit as well as the skills Dogged and NWI. 

Sometimes perks or armor may call for an increase or decrease in a unit's Wounds Skill. This is an incremental change that follows this path: 

`Dogged → NWI → +1 VITA/STR` 

* If a unit has neither Dogged nor NWI and gains a Wounds Skill they will gain Dogged
* If they have Dogged they lose Dogged and gain NWI
* If they have NWI they will lose NWI and increase their Wounds attribute by one

Wounds Skills can also be lost, reversing that path. A unit can never decrease their Wounds Skill below 1 VITA/STR.
