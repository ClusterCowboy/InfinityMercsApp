When a unit gains any Skills or modifications to their attributes (E.g. Perks, Armor Bonuses, etc.) they are <u>only</u> applied to the first profile of a **Transmutation (X)** trooper.

Additionally, any full VITA/STR increases will increase the Transmutation value by an equivalent amount. Thus gaining 1 full VITA would increase the Transmutation number by 1.

For a **Transmutation (WIP)** or **(Hatching)** trooper it is applied to the profile they transform into. 

Non Armor equipment such as weapons are available to both profiles. Additionally, use the transformed profile for determining any requirements for perks or equipment.

There is **one exception** to this rule. Neurocinetics applies to both profiles to prevent the ability to ignore the active turn drawback to the skill.
